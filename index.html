about us

24 years of experience


Shri Kishor Mangal Murti High School & Jr. College is one of the largest educational chains established in 2000, and having presence in Maharastra State. Shri Kishor Mangal Murti High School & Jr. College is a rising chain, with a network of more than 11000 students and about 600 teachers, all with a bent for achieving a better tomorrow. Every student at Shri Kishor Mangal Murti High School & Jr. College is nurtured to be a well-spoken and responsible citizen and is encouraged to dream ahead.

SKMMHC transforms students' dreams to reality through effectively designed curriculum, relevant to today's world. Dream India is a dream to see an Educated and Empowered India!
